    Release of WebSDK Technology Preview 1 - And Webpy Studio

    I've been steadily working on a prototype that is focused in RAD (Rapid Activity Development).

    For this I've made a selection of components and bundled them into a framework.

    To showcase the potential, I've rapidly developed a simple IDE (Integrated Development Environment) called Webpy.

    This bundle is meant as a starting point for developing complex and rich visual activities.

    Its all experimental stuff, nothing is set in stone yet. Feedback welcome.

    Thanks to Agustin Zubiaga for the Webkit binary component for XO laptops.

    Git Repository http://git.sugarlabs.org/websdk
